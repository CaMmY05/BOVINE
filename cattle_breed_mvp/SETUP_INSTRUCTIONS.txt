================================================================================
STEP 6: TRAIN THE MODEL
================================================================================

Train the breed classification model:
  python scripts/train_classifier.py

Training parameters:
  - Model: EfficientNet-B0
  - Epochs: 30
  - Batch Size: 32
  - Optimizer: AdamW
  - Learning Rate: 0.001

Expected training time:
  - With RTX 4000 Ada: ~5-10 minutes
  - CPU only: ~1-2 hours

The model will be saved to:
  models/classification/breed_classifier_v1/best_model.pth

================================================================================

This generates:

Results saved to: results/evaluation/

================================================================================
TROUBLESHOOTING
================================================================================

Problem: "No module named 'torch'"
Solution: pip install torch torchvision --index-url https://download.pytorch.org/whl/cu118

Problem: "CUDA out of memory"
Solution: Reduce batch_size in train_classifier.py (try 16 or 8)

Problem: "No cattle detected"
Solution: Lower confidence threshold or check image quality

Problem: Virtual environment not activating
Solution: Set-ExecutionPolicy -ExecutionPolicy RemoteSigned -Scope CurrentUser

Problem: Low accuracy
Solution: 
  - Collect more data (100+ images per breed)
  - Train for more epochs
  - Ensure data quality
